Overview

As a part of my Embedded Systems class I was assigned a project to design a system incorporating an embedded microcontroller (the MSP430). My team chose to design a robotic arm which could autonomously feed a person soup.

Motivation

My team came up with this project idea as a potential solution for assisting individuals who have difficulty feeding themselves, such as amputees or those with cerebral palsy. The assigned constraint for this project was that we needed to make use of an MSP430 microcontroller, and the final product needed to be powered directly from mains or with a battery.
